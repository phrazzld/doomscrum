# Adopt the Misty Step comic-ops aesthetic baseline

Priority: P3 · Status: pending · Estimate: M

## Goal
Evaluate whether Doomscrum should adopt the comic-ops baseline for sprint,
ritual, status, or retro surfaces.

## Oracle
- [ ] A design note names the chosen flavor and the specific product surfaces,
      or records a deliberate no-adoption decision.
- [ ] One representative sprint/retro/status surface is rendered or mocked with
      hard frames, caption strips, and restrained spot color.
- [ ] The design does not make operational status harder to scan.
- [ ] The implementation uses `@misty-step/aesthetic` commit `9bbe0f9` or later
      if adopted.
- [ ] The repo's canonical gate passes after implementation.
