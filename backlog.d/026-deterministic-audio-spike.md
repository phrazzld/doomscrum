# Spike: deterministic audio path (TTS + cheap silent video)

Priority: P3 · Status: pending · Estimate: M

## Goal
Prove a render path where the voiceover is generated by TTS (deterministic,
~$0.03/clip) over cheap silent video, eliminating the silent-dud and
garbled-diction re-roll classes entirely.

## Oracle
- [ ] One feed clip produced end-to-end for under $0.50 with a COMPLETE
      verdict from scripts/check_script_fit.py on the first roll.
- [ ] The pipeline records both audio and video provenance in the render
      JSON.

## Notes
Research 2026-06-10: Kokoro/ElevenLabs TTS at $0.02–0.05 per clip +
pixverse/wan silent video. Tradeoff: no lip-synced talking heads unless a
lipsync model is added — fine for narrator-style formats (italian opera,
infomercial VO), wrong for talking-head formats. **Why:** the two seedance
re-roll classes we pay for are both audio-nondeterminism; this removes the
nondeterminism instead of re-rolling it.
